ABOUT THE CLINIC

Verma Health Care Centre since its inception in 1995 has been at the forefront of Dental Healthcare, committed to providing world-class dental treatment at affordable rates. The clinic aims at offering different specialties of dentistry like Orthodontics, Endodontics, Periodontics, Prosthodontics, Cosmetic Dentistry,Oral Pythology, Oral surgery, Pediatric surgery and Restorative Dentistry all under one roof.
